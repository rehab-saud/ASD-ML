ASD Prediction for toddlers is screening questions that ask a series of 10 questions,
about the common behaviours of autism that appear on toddlers. It is intended for toddlers
between age 12 and 30 months of age, if your child score more than 3, then there is
a probability if the child has ASD traits otherwise not.
You can consult the healthcare providers about the result.

Please answer all the questions of the unusual behaviours of toddlers.
Your answer should be either 1 or 0, which means 1 equal to Yes and 0 equal to No.
